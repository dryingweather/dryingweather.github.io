About

Drying Weather are a Dublin based rock band that play original music.

Formed in 2019, they found their voice during the Coronavirus pandemic and wrote the majority of their music from a distance. They took advantage of the short oportunities between lockdowns to record at The Hive Studios and film short videos along the way. With the easing of restrictions in the near future, they plan on playing gigs in and around Dublin.
